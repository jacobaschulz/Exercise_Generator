This is a simplified version of the Exercise Generator.

Clicking the button above will generate one exercise from a library of exercises.

  • On a larger scale, not having to remember exercises is easier on an individual. This will choose an exercise for them. They won't have to remember a list of exercises.

  • With muscles and the body adapting to the stress of an exercise, this can lead to muscle stagnation in the long term. This serves to help move past that by choosing various exercises in a random order to promote muscle growth.
